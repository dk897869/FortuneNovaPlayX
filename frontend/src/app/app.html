💡 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I claim payouts?

Winning tokens or cashouts are automatically validated on the backend. Multipliers are instantly credited to your wallet balance.

Is the gaming outcome provably fair?

Yes! Every roll of the dice or mine coordinate is determined using HMAC-SHA256 calculations containing revealed server seeds and your client seeds. You can verify outcome integrity using the verifier at the bottom of the Dashboard page.

How long do deposits and withdrawals take?

Deposits process instantly. Withdrawals to UPI, Paytm, or Bank Accounts are simulated in sandbox mode and process immediately upon request submission.
